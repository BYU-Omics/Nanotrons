    ABOUT US

    Nanotrons is a user interface made by Electrical Engineering students of Brigham Young University for its Chemical Engineering department and the Kelly lab. We have taken the Opentrons sample preparing robot with its subsequent coding and made a more user-friendly interface that allows for anyone, whether they are experienced chemists or beggininers, to be able to use the Opentron robot and prepare their samples. Further more, our interface takes a new approach compared to the opentron interface; therefore we have made a guide for new users to get familiar with our program, which will be posted in this page soon!!
